Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Things to Do France uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at thingstodofrance.com. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them to enhance your travel planning experience.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information that helps us show you the most relevant French destinations.

How we use cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our website to operate. Other cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our Online Property, such as remembering your preferred cities.

Third-party cookies (Advertising & Analytics)

Third parties serve cookies through our website for advertising, analytics, and other purposes. This includes Google AdSense, which uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet to provide you with relevant travel offers.

Type of Cookie Purpose
Essential Strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our website and use its features.
Advertising Used by Google AdSense to make advertising messages more relevant to your travel interests.
Analytics Help us understand how our website is being used, so we can improve our city guides.

How can I control cookies?

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.

To opt-out of Google's use of cookies for personalized advertising, you can visit Google Ads Settings.
