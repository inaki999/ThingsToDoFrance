About the Project

Your Ultimate 2026 France Travel Guide

Welcome to THINGS TO DO FRANCE. This project was born from a passion for travel and a deep love for French culture, designed specifically to help you navigate the beautiful landscapes of France in 2026.

Our mission at thingstodofrance.com is simple: to provide a visual, intuitive, and highly curated experience for travelers. We believe that planning a trip should be as inspiring as the journey itself. That’s why we’ve gathered the best "Must-Visit" spots and "Must-Eat" locations in one single place.

Whether you are looking for the medieval charm of Annecy, the mechanical wonders of Nantes, or the sunny terraces of Montpellier, this guide is built to save you time and provide hand-picked inspiration for every type of traveler.
