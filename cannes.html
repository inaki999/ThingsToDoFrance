Cannes 2026

Cinema, Glamour & Mediterranean Blue

Top 10 Architectural & Scenic Sites in Cannes

La Croisette

Iconic palm-lined promenade featuring luxury boutiques, grand hotels, and golden beaches.

💡 Tip: Walk the entire length at sunset for the most glamourous photos.

Le Suquet

The charming old town of Cannes with cobblestone streets and spectacular panoramic views.

💡 Tip: Visit at night when the narrow alleys are beautifully lit and full of life.

Palais des Festivals

The world-famous venue for the Cannes Film Festival and its iconic red-carpet stairs.

💡 Tip: Look for the handprints of movie stars on the "Chemin des Étoiles" nearby.

Lérins Islands

Saint-Honorat and Sainte-Marguerite islands offer pristine nature just a short boat ride away.

💡 Tip: Visit the fort on Sainte-Marguerite where the "Man in the Iron Mask" was held.

Notre-Dame d’Espérance

Historic Gothic church located at the top of Le Suquet, offering the best views of the bay.

💡 Tip: The square in front of the church is perfect for a 360-degree city photo.

Marché Forville

A famous covered market filled with fresh local flowers, seasonal produce, and Niçois snacks.

💡 Tip: Go early in the morning to see the local chefs buying their ingredients.

Place de la Castre

A beautiful viewpoint and ancient castle housing the Museum of World Explorations.

💡 Tip: Climb the tower of the castle for an even higher vantage point.

Plage du Midi

A long, relaxed public beach west of the port, popular with locals and sunseekers.

💡 Tip: Great for a picnic at dusk away from the Croisette crowds.

Villa Domergue

An exquisite Art Deco villa with Mediterranean gardens inspired by the Italian style.

💡 Tip: Check for seasonal art exhibitions held in its historic rooms.

Plage de la Croisette

The famous strip of beaches along the main promenade, featuring both public and private clubs.

💡 Tip: Many private beach clubs offer a sunset cocktail without needing a full bed rental.

Top 10 Culinary Experiences in Cannes

La Palme d’Or

The legendary 2-star Michelin restaurant at Hotel Martinez. High-end cinema-inspired dining.

💰 Price: Luxury. Peak fine dining on the French Riviera.

Astoux et Brun

A Cannes institution famous for its incredibly fresh seafood platters and oysters by the port.

💰 Price: Mid-range / Casual-Fine. A must for seafood lovers.

Le Bistrot Gourmand

Authentic and creative French cuisine served in a warm, traditional bistro atmosphere.

💰 Price: Cheap / Casual. Excellent value for high-quality food.

La Pizza Cresci

An iconic pizzeria in the heart of Cannes, known for its massive, delicious wood-fired pizzas.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. Perfect for a casual dinner.

Bobo Bistro

Modern Mediterranean cuisine in a trendy, artsy setting. Very popular with the local crowd.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Great atmosphere and creative plates.

L’Oasis

Located just outside Cannes in Mandelieu, this 2-star Michelin jewel offers a magical garden setting.

💰 Price: Luxury. An unforgettable gourmet garden experience.

Mantel

Elegant and smart dining in the Old Town, specializing in contemporary French flavors.

💰 Price: High-end. Refined cooking in the heart of Le Suquet.

Burger & Beef

Gourmet burgers made with premium ingredients for a high-quality yet casual meal.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. The best burger spot in town.

Café Roma

Authentic Italian cuisine using fresh local products, located right across the Palais des Festivals.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Perfect for people-watching and pasta.

Le Park 45

Located in the Grand Hotel, it offers elegant and creative cuisine in a stunning park-side setting.

💰 Price: High-end. Sophisticated dining near the Croisette.

Ultimate Cannes Travel Itineraries – 1, 3 & 7 Day Guides

⏱️ 1-Day Express

The Best of Cannes in 24 Hours

  • 09:00: Morning stroll along La Croisette under the palm trees.
  • 10:30: Climb up to Le Suquet and visit Notre-Dame d’Espérance for the views.
  • 12:30: Seafood lunch at Astoux et Brun near the port.
  • 14:30: Red carpet photo at Palais des Festivals and relax at Plage du Midi.
  • 17:00: Snack and local products at Marché Forville.
  • 19:30: Grand dinner at La Palme d’Or or Mantel.
🥂 Weekend Getaway

3-Day Riviera Glamour

  • Day 1: Explore Le Suquet, Marché Forville and sunset at La Croisette.
  • Day 2: Boat trip to Îles de Lérins (Sainte-Marguerite). Picnic and fortress visit.
  • Day 3: Luxury shopping in the center, pizza at Cresci, and a afternoon at a beach club.
🏰 7-Day Immersion

The Full French Riviera Experience

  • Day 1: Cannes highlights – Croisette, Suquet, and dinner at Astoux et Brun.
  • Day 2: Nature day at Lérins Islands – hiking and swimming.
  • Day 3: Culture morning at Villa Domergue and Castre Museum.
  • Day 4: Day trip to Antibes (Picasso Museum & Port Vauban).
  • Day 5: Day trip to Monaco – Palace and Monte Carlo Casino.
  • Day 6: Nice highlights – Promenade des Anglais and Old Town.
  • Day 7: Relaxing beach day and farewell dinner at La Palme d’Or.
