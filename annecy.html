Annecy 2026

Alpine Magic: Crystal Waters & Medieval Canals

Top 10 Must-Visit Places in Annecy

Lake Annecy

One of the cleanest lakes in Europe, offering breathtaking Alpine views and crystal waters.

💡 Tip: Rent a small boat or a paddleboard to truly experience the silence of the lake.

Vieille Ville (Old Town)

A fairy-tale maze of cobblestone streets, colorful houses, and narrow flower-lined canals.

💡 Tip: Visit early in the morning to enjoy the architecture before the crowds arrive.

Palais de l’Isle

An iconic 12th-century "house in the shape of a ship," formerly a prison and courthouse.

💡 Tip: This is the most photographed spot in the city—best shots from the Perrière bridge.

Château d’Annecy

A restored castle overlooking the city, housing a museum of regional art and history.

💡 Tip: The climb is steep, but the terrace offers a perfect panoramic view of the roofs.

Jardins de l’Europe

A lakeside English-style park with ancient trees, perfect for a picnic or a quiet stroll.

💡 Tip: Look for the sundial and the century-old giant sequoias.

Pont des Amours

The "Lover's Bridge," a beautiful iron bridge connecting the gardens to the Pâquier field.

💡 Tip: Legend says if you kiss in the middle, you will stay together forever.

Semnoz Hill

A natural viewpoint offering green trails in summer and snowy slopes in winter.

💡 Tip: Drive to the summit for a 360° view of Mont Blanc on clear days.

Plage d’Albigny

A large, free public beach with grass and shallow waters, ideal for sunbathing and swimming.

💡 Tip: It's the most popular beach, so arrive before noon during July and August.

Saint-Maurice Church

A historic 15th-century Gothic church with impressive architecture and religious art.

💡 Tip: Check for local choir concerts occasionally held inside.

Le Thiou Canal

A picturesque walk along the canal featuring old mills and quiet promenades away from the center.

💡 Tip: Follow the path all the way to Cran-Gevrier for a more local, less touristy vibe.

Top 10 Culinary Stops in Annecy

Le Clos des Sens

A 2-star Michelin powerhouse focusing on lake-to-table cuisine and garden herbs.

💰 Price: Luxury. Booking months in advance is essential.

La Table d’Édouard

Sophisticated and elegant French cooking with a strong focus on seasonal Alpine products.

💰 Price: Luxury. Fine dining at its best.

L’Etage

Creative modern French cuisine served in a stylish second-floor setting in the Old Town.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Excellent flavor combinations.

Le Bistrot du Port

Fresh Mediterranean-inspired dishes with a prime location overlooking the harbor.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Great for a sunny lunch.

Le Belvédère

A fantastic restaurant offering high-quality dishes and stunning views of the lake.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Great value for the view.

Le Freti

The place to go for authentic Savoyard Fondue, Raclette, and Tartiflette.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. Expect queues, but it's worth it!

Chez Mamie Lise

A cozy, traditionally decorated bistro serving classic French Alpine comfort food.

💰 Price: Budget / Mid-range. Very warm atmosphere.

Pizzéria Riva

Delicious thin-crust pizzas and fresh pasta served right next to the canal.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. Perfect for families.

La Boîte à Sardine

Fresh local market ingredients served in a simple, casual, and tasty way.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. Honest and delicious cooking.

Le Bilboquet

A charming spot for quality sandwiches, artisanal coffee, and light bites in the heart of town.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. Perfect for a quick break.

Annecy Travel Itineraries – 1, 3 & 7 Day Guides

⏱️ 1-Day Highlights

Annecy's Best in a Day

09:00: Old Town Walk – Coffee at Place Sainte-Claire followed by a stroll.
10:30: Palais de l’Isle – Visit the iconic landmark and the history museum.
12:30: Lunch at Le Freti – Indulge in an authentic Savoyard Fondue.
14:00: Lake Annecy Promenade – Walk along the Quai or rent a bike for the shore.
16:00: Pont des Amours & Gardens – Relax by the water and take photos.
19:00: Dinner at Le Bistrot du Port – Fresh fish with harbor views.

🥂 Weekend Escape

3 Days: Lakes & Mountains

Day 1: Historical Old Town. Palais de l’Isle, Gardens, and dinner at L’Etage.
Day 2: Peaks & Beaches. Morning hike at Semnoz, afternoon at Albigny Beach. Dinner at Le Belvédère.
Day 3: Culture & Fine Dining. Visit the Château, take a boat cruise, and a gourmet farewell at La Table d’Édouard.

🏔️ 7-Day Alpine Adventure

The Ultimate Savoyard Week

Day 1: Arrival & Old Town exploration.
Day 2: Hiking day at Semnoz with a summit picnic.
Day 3: Water sports day. Kayaking or Paddleboarding on the lake.
Day 4: Alpine Village Day Trip. Visit nearby Talloires or the castle at Duingt.
Day 5: Local Culture. Visit the Château d’Annecy and the local farmers market.
Day 6: Relaxation Day. Sunbathing at Albigny Beach and café hopping.
Day 7: Grand Finale. Luxury dinner at the world-renowned Le Clos des Sens.
