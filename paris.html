Paris 2026

Architecture, Gastronomy & Detailed Travel Itineraries

Top 10 Architectural Sites in Paris

Eiffel Tower

Constructed in 1889, the Eiffel Tower is Paris' most iconic landmark, a symbol of French innovation and stunning city views.

💡 Tip: Visit at sunset for the best skyline photos. Book tickets online to skip the queue.

Louvre Museum

Home to over 38,000 artworks including the Mona Lisa, the Louvre combines history, art, and architecture in the heart of Paris.

💡 Tip: Start early at the Denon Wing to see masterpieces with fewer crowds.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of Gothic architecture on the Île de la Cité, famous for its flying buttresses, gargoyles, and historical significance.

💡 Tip: Explore the nearby crypt and square for quiet photo spots.

Arc de Triomphe

Commissioned by Napoleon, this monumental arch honors French victories and offers a panoramic view of the Champs-Élysées.

💡 Tip: Climb to the top at dusk to watch the city lights illuminate Paris’ main avenues.

Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Sitting atop Montmartre, this Romano-Byzantine basilica is famous for its white domes and breathtaking views of Paris.

💡 Tip: Visit early morning for a peaceful experience and better light for photos.

Orsay Museum

Housed in a former Beaux-Arts train station, the Orsay Museum features the world’s finest Impressionist and Post-Impressionist collections.

💡 Tip: Check the temporary exhibitions—they often highlight rare masterpieces.

Pompidou Centre

Known for its radical inside-out design, the Pompidou Centre houses contemporary art, design, and cultural exhibitions.

💡 Tip: Take the external escalators to enjoy a city view from above.

Sainte-Chapelle

This Gothic chapel is renowned for its vibrant stained-glass windows, depicting biblical stories in intricate detail.

💡 Tip: Visit on a sunny day to see the windows illuminate with natural light.

Palace of Versailles

A symbol of royal grandeur, Versailles dazzles with the Hall of Mirrors, formal gardens, and opulent architecture.

💡 Tip: Allocate a full day; rent a golf cart to explore the expansive gardens.

Luxembourg Gardens

A historical park combining French and English garden styles, perfect for a stroll, picnic, or people-watching in central Paris.

💡 Tip: Visit early to enjoy quiet paths and photograph the Medici Fountain.

Top 10 Culinary Experiences in Paris

Le Meurice

Experience the best luxury dining in Paris 2026. Alain Ducasse crafts a high-end Michelin-star experience in a historic palace.

💰 Price: Luxury dining. Ideal for gourmet travelers in Paris.

L’Ambroisie

A true icon of fine dining in Paris. It is widely considered the top luxury restaurant in Paris for classic French gastronomy.

💰 Price: Luxury / Exclusive. Premier high-end journey in France.

Maison Chocolat

For those seeking affordable luxury treats in Paris, this shop is the gold standard for premium gourmet gifts.

💰 Price: Moderate. High-quality chocolates at a mid-range price.

Enfants Rouges

One of the best cheap restaurants in Paris. This historic market is perfect for budget-friendly local food in 2026.

💰 Price: Cheap & Budget-friendly. Best for cheap eats in Paris.

Café de Flore

Visit this famous café for a mid-range dining option in Paris. Iconic stop for travelers seeking authentic French cafe culture.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Essential experience in Saint-Germain.

Pierre Hermé

Discover the best macarons in Paris. It offers an affordable luxury pastry experience with world-renowned techniques.

💰 Price: Moderate. High-quality French desserts on a mid-range budget.

Comptoir Relais

A top-rated French bistro in Paris. It serves market-fresh classics, making it a top mid-range restaurant in Saint-Germain.

💰 Price: Mid-range. Excellent value for traditional bistro cuisine.

Berthillon

Searching for cheap treats in Paris? This is officially the best affordable ice cream in Paris, located in Île Saint-Louis.

💰 Price: Budget-friendly. Famous but cheap desserts in Paris.

Breizh Café

One of the best cheap restaurants in Paris for crepes. High-quality galettes for top-tier budget-friendly dining.

💰 Price: Cheap / Moderate. Best value-for-money eats in Paris.

Guy Savoy

The ultimate luxury dining experience in Paris. Consistently ranked as one of the best Michelin-star restaurants in the world.

💰 Price: Luxury / Fine Dining. Peak of French gastronomy in 2026.

Ultimate Paris Travel Itineraries – 1, 3 & 7 Day Guides

⏱️ 1-Day Express

Best of Paris in a Day

09:00: Eiffel Tower – enjoy morning light and panoramic views.
11:00: Walk along the Seine to the Louvre – quick visit to the must-see artworks (Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo).
13:30: Lunch in Le Marais – try local cafés and bistros.
15:00: Notre-Dame & Île de la Cité – explore the cathedral and surroundings.
16:30: Sainte-Chapelle – see the stained-glass windows illuminated by sunlight.
18:00: Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur – sunset views of Paris.
20:00: Evening Seine stroll or dinner near the Eiffel Tower.

🥂 Weekend Getaway

3-Day Paris Highlights

Day 1: Morning at the Eiffel Tower & Champ de Mars, visit Trocadéro. Lunch in Saint-Germain. Afternoon stroll along the Seine and dinner in Le Marais.
Day 2: Louvre – focus on the classics, then Tuileries Garden and Place de la Concorde. Lunch in Le Marais. Afternoon at Musée d'Orsay. Dinner in Saint-Germain or near the Opera.
Day 3: Montmartre – Sacré-Cœur, Place du Tertre, local cafés, street art. Optional exterior visit to Moulin Rouge. Afternoon free for shopping in Le Marais or a walk along Canal Saint-Martin.

🏰 7-Day Immersion

Complete Week in Paris

Day 1: Eiffel Tower & Champ de Mars, Trocadéro, Seine stroll.
Day 2: Louvre – masterpieces, Tuileries Garden, Place de la Concorde, Palais Royal.
Day 3: Musée d'Orsay, Saint-Germain-des-Prés, historic cafés, typical brunch.
Day 4: Montmartre – Sacré-Cœur, Place du Tertre, street art, Moulin Rouge (exterior).
Day 5: Pompidou Centre & contemporary art, Marais for shopping and culture, dinner at a typical bistro.
Day 6: Excursion to Versailles – Palace, Gardens, Grand Trianon.
Day 7: Excursion to Giverny (Monet’s Garden), relaxing afternoon at Luxembourg Gardens, farewell dinner at Guy Savoy or another iconic restaurant.
